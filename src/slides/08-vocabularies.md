## Homosaurus

[Homosaurus](https://homosaurus.org/) is a linked data vocabulary focusing on LGBTQ+ terms. "This vocabulary is intended to function as a companion to broad subject term vocabularies, such as the Library of Congress Subject Headings."

## Getty

The Getty Research Institute maintains [a set of vocabularies](https://www.getty.edu/research/tools/vocabularies/) which are "structured resources for the visual arts domain, including art, architecture, decorative arts, other cultural works, archival materials, visual surrogates, and art conservation."

## Wikidata

[Wikidata](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Wikidata:Main_Page) is a **massive** "free and open knowledge base that can be read and edited by both humans and machines." It stores structured data to support Wikipedia and has no topical focus.
