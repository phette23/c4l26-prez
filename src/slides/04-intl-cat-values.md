---

"Gulf of America" is difficult to justify in terms of <em>cataloging values</em>. Consider IFLA's "[Statement of International Cataloguing Principles](https://www.ifla.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/assets/cataloguing/icp/icp_2016-en.pdf)": Convenience of the user, Common usage, Consistency and standardization, Interoperability, and Rationality.
