title: "Cataloging Values"
---

"Gulf of America" is difficult to justify in terms of <em>cataloging values</em>. Consider a few principles from <abbr title="International Federation of Library Associations and Institutions">IFLA</abbr>'s "[Statement of International Cataloguing Principles](https://www.ifla.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/assets/cataloguing/icp/icp_2016-en.pdf)":

- Convenience of the user
- Common usage
- Economy
- Consistency and standardization
- Interoperability
- Rationality
